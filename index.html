Snow White and The Seven Dwarfs (1937)

There is no better movie to start with than the first animated Disney film, Snow White. The movie has it all from a winsome protagonist, goofy sidekicks, and a terrifying-looking villain. The film is most known for the multiplane camera captures scenes of the Evil Queen's castle and creature filled forest. These animation cels captured complex shots, making it one of the most well-known Disney fairy tales to exist.

Fantasia (1940)

Fantasia is unlike any film Disney has done before. As you watch, you will notice how there is little dialogue and only a few characters. Animators truly had to do a lot of experimenting with the dancing flowers and mushrooms.

If not, watch the trailer for this classic tale!
